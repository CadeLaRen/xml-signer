GENI Authorization Tool

The GENI Authorization Tool allows you to authorize applications to speak on your behalf to allocate slices or slivers. Your GENI certificate and private key are stored in your web browser and your passphrase is never transmitted over the network.

To authorize, enter the passphrase for your GENI private key below. Once you authorize, the tool will be able to act on your behalf when talking to GENI infrastructure. Only authorize if you trust the tool.
